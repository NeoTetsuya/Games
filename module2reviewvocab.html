Passage 1: Family & Society

In modern, societies, family structures have evolved significantly. While many young people value their and prefer to live in families consisting only of parents and children, others still maintain strong ties with their families. Regardless of the setup, sharing household remains a crucial aspect of maintaining a harmonious home environment.

Passage 2: Traditions & History

Throughout history, communities have gathered to significant seasonal changes and historical events. These vibrant festivals usually annually and are deeply rooted in local tradition. A common during these celebrations involves the warmly welcoming friends and family into their home, ensuring that all are treated to traditional food and entertainment.
